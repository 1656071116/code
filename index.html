震惊！南浦老师语言通俗易懂

老刘名言:

  • 傻得要命
    • Stupid to death.
  • 屎掉裤裆里不知道臭的
    • I don't know if it stinks in my crotch.
  • 无丝竹之乱耳，无案牍之劳形
    • There is no disordered ear of silk and bamboo, and there is no work of writing.
  • 有问题找等边三角形
    • If you have a problem, please find the equilateral triangle.
  • 死一片的
    • Died a slice.
  • 都流到东海里去了
    • It's all flowing into the East China Sea.
  • 钉钉子转角
    • Nail corner.
  • 没有谈头
    • No talk.

刚哥名言：

    • Since come, it's safe.

    • God laughs as soon as human are thinking.
  • 我从来不记仇，只是当场报复
    • I never take revenge. I just take revenge on the spot.
